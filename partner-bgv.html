Partner BGV Application

Partner Background Verification (BGV) Application is a secure, compliance-driven platform designed to handle sensitive partner onboarding and verification workflows at PwC India.

Security UX Compliance Enterprise Platform Risk Management

The product required a balance between strict verification requirements and a frictionless experience for senior stakeholders.

Problem Statement

  • Highly sensitive data with zero tolerance for errors
  • Complex multi-step verification flows
  • Manual follow-ups and status ambiguity
  • Low visibility into verification progress

Security & Compliance Context

Partner BGV operates under heightened compliance and audit requirements due to the seniority and legal responsibilities of partners.

  • Identity verification and background checks
  • Document authenticity and validation
  • Audit trails and access controls
  • Data privacy and retention policies

My Role

I served as the Senior UI/UX Designer, responsible for designing a secure yet usable experience.

  • User flow simplification
  • Designing secure form interactions
  • Status tracking and transparency
  • Stakeholder reviews and iteration
  • Design system alignment

Research & Discovery

  • Workshops with compliance teams
  • Understanding audit and legal requirements
  • Review of existing BGV tools
  • Risk analysis across user touchpoints
Compliance Mapping · Risk Areas · User Anxiety Points

UX Strategy

  • Step-based guided workflows
  • Clear progress indicators
  • Inline validation and feedback
  • Minimal cognitive load per step

Design Execution

  • Secure document upload flows
  • Status-driven dashboards
  • Audit-friendly layouts
  • Role-based access patterns
