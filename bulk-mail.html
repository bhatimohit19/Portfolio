Bulk Mail Application

Bulk Mail Application is an internal PwC platform for efficiently sending announcements, newsletters, and notifications to employees and partners.

Workflow UX Notifications Enterprise Tool Email Management

The goal was to streamline message creation, scheduling, and delivery while providing visibility into engagement and errors.

Problem Statement

  • Manual and error-prone mail dispatch process
  • High cognitive load for admins managing multiple audiences
  • Lack of visibility on mail delivery and engagement
  • Limited analytics and reporting capabilities

Business Context

In a global consulting firm, clear and timely communication is crucial. Bulk mail errors can lead to compliance issues, missed deadlines, and confusion among employees.

  • HR announcements and policy updates
  • Internal campaigns and newsletters
  • Tracking engagement and delivery performance
  • Minimizing errors while scaling communication

My Role

As Senior UI/UX Designer, I focused on simplifying complex bulk mail workflows for internal stakeholders.

  • User journey mapping and workflow simplification
  • Information architecture for mail templates
  • UI design of scheduling, preview, and analytics screens
  • Validation and error prevention patterns
  • Iterative feedback sessions with HR and IT admins

Research & Discovery

  • Interviews with HR and admin teams
  • Audit of existing mail processes
  • Competitor analysis of internal communication tools
  • Analytics review to identify key pain points
Personas · Workflow Maps · Error Patterns · Admin Needs

UX Strategy

  • Step-based workflow for mail creation
  • Live preview before scheduling
  • Inline error handling and notifications
  • Analytics dashboard for engagement tracking

Design Execution

  • Template management and reusable components
  • Recipient segmentation and filtering UI
  • Scheduling, confirmation, and error notifications
  • Analytics dashboard with charts and delivery stats
Bulk Mail Screens · Analytics Charts · Template Flow

  • Analytics dashboards drive adoption and visibility
