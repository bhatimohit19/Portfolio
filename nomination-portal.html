Nomination Portal

A centralized enterprise platform designed to manage employee nominations, background verification (BGV), statutory declarations, and UAN submissions within PwC India.

Enterprise UX Compliance HR Tech Internal Platform

This project focused on transforming fragmented, compliance-heavy workflows into a guided, transparent experience while maintaining audit readiness.

Problem Statement

  • Multiple systems and email-based processes
  • High form errors and incomplete submissions
  • Heavy HR dependency for corrections
  • Lack of real-time status visibility

Enterprise & Compliance Context

  • Statutory compliance (BGV, UAN, declarations)
  • Role-based access (Employee, HR Ops, Compliance)
  • Audit logs and traceability
  • Integration with internal HR systems and vendors

My Role

I worked as the Senior UI/UX Designer owning the complete experience design lifecycle.

  • Stakeholder alignment with HR, Payroll, Compliance
  • User flows and information architecture
  • Wireframing, UI design, prototyping
  • Usability validation and design handoff

Research & Discovery

  • Stakeholder interviews
  • Support ticket & error analysis
  • Workflow walkthroughs
  • Form usability audits
Research Notes · Pain Points · Error Patterns

UX Strategy

  • Step-based guided forms
  • Progress indicators
  • Inline validation
  • Status-driven transparency

Design Execution

  • Reusable enterprise form components
  • Clear document upload guidance
  • Consistent PwC-aligned UI patterns
  • Accessibility-aware layouts
Wireframes · UI Screens · Component Library
