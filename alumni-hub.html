Alumni Hub

Alumni Hub is an engagement platform designed to maintain long-term relationships between PwC India and its former employees.

Engagement UX Community Platform Employer Branding Internal Product

The platform supports networking, rehiring opportunities, knowledge sharing, and brand advocacy.

Problem Statement

  • No centralized alumni engagement channel
  • Disconnected communication via emails and events
  • Missed rehiring and referral opportunities
  • Low post-exit brand engagement

Business Context

For large consulting organizations like PwC, alumni represent long-term brand ambassadors and potential future hires.

  • Employer branding and reputation
  • Talent pipeline and boomerang hiring
  • Community-driven engagement
  • Content governance and moderation

My Role

I worked as the Senior UI/UX Designer responsible for shaping the alumni engagement experience.

  • Experience strategy and content structure
  • User journeys and interaction design
  • High-fidelity UI design
  • Usability validation
  • Design handoff and iteration

Research & Discovery

  • Interviews with HR and alumni relations teams
  • Feedback from former employees
  • Benchmarking alumni platforms
  • Content consumption analysis
Personas · Engagement Patterns · Content Insights

UX Strategy

  • Content-first homepage
  • Clear value proposition for alumni
  • Simple onboarding and profile setup
  • Personalized content and opportunities

Design Execution

  • Event-driven and story-based layouts
  • Job and referral discovery modules
  • Accessible and welcoming UI
  • Scalable component-based design
Alumni Hub Screens · Event Pages · Content Cards

Key Learnings

  • Engagement platforms must deliver ongoing value
  • Community UX relies on trust and relevance
  • Strong content hierarchy drives retention
