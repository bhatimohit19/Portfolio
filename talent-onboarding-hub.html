Talent Onboarding Hub (TOH)

Gamified Onboarding Platform for PwC India

TOH engages new joiners with interactive quizzes, challenges, badges, and dashboards. Designed to increase knowledge retention, compliance adherence, and excitement during the first 90 days of joining.

Back to Portfolio

Project Summary

Role: Senior UI/UX Designer

Duration: 3 Months

Tools: Figma, Adobe XD, Miro, Google Analytics

TOH is an internal gamified onboarding platform designed for new joiners at PwC India. The system combines learning, engagement, and compliance tracking in a seamless experience.
