Payroll Platform

An enterprise payroll platform designed to manage salary processing, deductions, statutory compliance, and reporting for PwC India.

Enterprise UX Payroll Compliance Data-heavy UI

This project focused on making complex payroll data transparent, trustworthy, and usable for both payroll administrators and employees.

Problem Statement

  • Payroll data presented in dense tables with poor hierarchy
  • Employees lacked clarity on salary components and deductions
  • High dependency on payroll support for explanations
  • Compliance terms were difficult to understand

Payroll & Compliance Context

Payroll systems operate under strict accuracy and compliance expectations. Even small errors impact employee trust.

  • Statutory components (PF, PT, TDS, allowances)
  • Monthly and annual payroll cycles
  • Audit readiness and reporting
  • Integration with finance and HR systems

My Role

I worked as the Senior UI/UX Designer responsible for simplifying complex payroll workflows.

  • Stakeholder collaboration with Payroll & Finance teams
  • User flow restructuring
  • Dashboard and data visualization design
  • Usability validation
  • Design handoff to development

Research & Discovery

  • Discussions with payroll administrators
  • Employee feedback on salary slips
  • Support ticket analysis
  • Comparative analysis of payroll systems
Research Insights · Pain Points · Data Confusion Areas

UX Strategy

  • Dashboard-first experience
  • Clear separation of earnings and deductions
  • Progressive disclosure for detailed data
  • Contextual help for payroll terminology

Design Execution

  • High-level payroll summary cards
  • Expandable tables for detailed breakdowns
  • Consistent data formatting
  • Accessible color coding for deductions
Payroll Dashboard · Salary Slip UI · Reports

  • Data hierarchy improves decision-making
